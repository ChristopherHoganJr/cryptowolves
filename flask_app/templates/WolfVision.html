Wolf Vision

The vision is simple, create a platform where educated calls can be made, discussed, and challenged. We are not here to make calls about random moonshots or pump and dump schemes.

User Privacy

We will only collect the information that you give us: your email and a few confirmations. We will not sell any information.

Security

All sensitive information has been encrypted before it even touches our databases. We don't know your passwords and we don't want to.

API Usage

All APIs are taken from CoinGecko. We do not collect market information, rather pull from a reputable source.
